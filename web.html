web development

VBIKES enables users to quickly find and connect with nearby mechanics for bike repairs and maintenance. Designed for ease of use, the feature enhances accessibility, ensuring riders can locate reliable mechanics anytime, anywhere.

key feature

  • Real-Time Mechanic Locator – View available mechanics nearby with live location tracking.
  • Instant Booking & Scheduling – Book immediate or scheduled service appointments with mechanics.
  • Mechanic Profiles & Reviews – Browse ratings, reviews, and expertise before choosing a service provider.
  • In-App Communication – Chat or call mechanics directly within the app
  • Service Cost Estimates – Get price estimates before confirming a booking.
  • Secure Digital Payments – Pay conveniently using in-app payment options.
